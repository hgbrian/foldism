What happens when MSA is off?
  • Boltz-2 — disabled (Boltz only runs with MSA).

  • OpenFold 3 — runs without MSA. Accuracy drops a lot.
  • Chai-1 — no MSA, falls back to ESM2 embeddings only. Moderate quality.
  • ESMFold 2 — no MSA, falls back to ESMC embeddings only. This is its intended fast mode.
